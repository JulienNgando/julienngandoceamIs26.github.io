This meeting proved to be incredibly valuable, as it helped clarify the next major milestones for my research and provided direction for the technical challenges ahead.
The most critical takeaway from the advisement session was discovering how machine learning techniques will be integrated into my project. Specifically, my immediate focus will shift toward extensive data engineering. I am going to be responsible for cleaning and merging complex datasets sourced from both satellite feeds and ground stations, setting a solid foundation for the upcoming ML implementation.
